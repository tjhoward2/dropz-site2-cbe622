Pokemon Go for Brands.

02 — Platform

An SDK. Not another app.

DROPZ is infrastructure delivered as an embeddable SDK. Partners ship spatial engagement inside their existing apps — campus apps, telecom apps, fan apps, media apps — in days, not quarters.

SDK · Reference experience

The experience your app can power.

DROPZ ships as an embeddable SDK. We don't compete with your app — we power it. The mockup shown is a reference of what a partner app, campus app, or telecom client can deliver to their users in days, using our map, drops, and rewards primitives.

05 — Digital Real Estate Thesis

A new asset class hiding in plain sight.

Physical inventory is finite. Digital inventory layered over physical space is expandable, programmable, and ownable.

// 01 — INVENTORY

Physical ad space is capped. Digital isn't.

You cannot add billboards to a stadium concourse. You can add infinite addressable digital drops to the same square foot — and re-program them daily.

// 02 — OWNERSHIP

Ownership of a digital layer.

DROPZ creates exclusive, durable ownership of the digital layer over real-world environments — campuses, venues, blocks — enforced through the platform.

// 03 — RECURRING

Locations generate recurring income.

Every active location becomes a recurring revenue node. Stack thousands of nodes and you have a portfolio of cash-flowing digital plots.

// 04 — MARGIN

Real estate economics. Software margins.

Comparable to commercial real estate, but with programmable inventory, software margins, and a near-zero marginal cost to add a new location.

// 05 — STRUCTURE

Foundation for a future DREIT.

As the network of locations and recurring contracts compounds, DROPZ provides the foundation for a Digital Real Estate Investment Trust — the first portfolio of programmable physical-world digital plots.
